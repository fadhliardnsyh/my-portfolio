My Process

How I design with purpose

A structured approach that turns ambiguity into clarity — and clarity into products people love.

01User Research

Everything starts with understanding people. I conduct interviews, usability tests, and competitive analysis to uncover real user needs, mental models, and pain points — before writing a single spec.

02Define

I synthesize research into clear problem statements, user personas, and journey maps. This phase transforms scattered insights into a focused design brief — the North Star for every decision that follows.

03Design

From rough sketches to high-fidelity prototypes, I craft interfaces that are intuitive, accessible, and visually coherent. I build design systems, not one-off screens — scalable, consistent, and developer-friendly.

04Test & Iterate

Design never ships perfect — it ships validated. I test prototypes with real users, gather feedback, and iterate fast. Every round brings the product closer to something people genuinely love.
