Overview

A smarter way to manage
human resources

Fixwork is a client-based HRIS (Human Resource Information System) mobile application designed to streamline workforce management for companies of all sizes. The app handles everything from attendance tracking and leave management to payroll and asset requests — all in one place.

With over 500+ active daily users, the challenge was to create an interface that's powerful enough for HR administrators, yet intuitive enough for everyday employees to use without training.

The Problem

HR processes were slow,
manual, and frustrating

Before Fixwork, companies relied on spreadsheets, physical forms, and manual approvals to manage their workforce. This created bottlenecks, errors, and a poor experience for both HR teams and employees.

01 — Challenge
Still relying on conventional fingerprint attendance
Companies using Fixwork previously relied on fingerprint scanners for attendance. In larger organizations, this created long queues — employees who arrived on time were marked late simply because of the line. A faster, individual-based check-in solution was urgently needed.
02 — Challenge
No visibility into personal attendance data
Attendance data — whether an employee was on time or late — was held exclusively by HR. Employees had no way to check their own records, which frequently led to conflicts between HR and employees over disputed tardiness that could have been avoided with simple transparency.
03 — Challenge
Manual and fragmented leave & permit requests
Leave and permit requests were still handled manually — employees had to personally approach their supervisor, then HR, going through multiple steps just to get approval. On top of that, employees had no way to track how many leave days they had left, leading to confusion and miscommunication.
04 — Challenge
Tedious manual process for out-of-office visits
When employees needed to visit a client or attend an external meeting, the process was painfully manual — they had to take a photo with GPS metadata enabled, then manually send it to HR as proof of location. No standardized flow, no real-time visibility, and highly prone to error or miscommunication.
05 — Challenge
Overtime requests still done the old-fashioned way
Overtime requests were handled conventionally — employees had to submit manually, often through paper forms or direct messages to their supervisor. There was no centralized system to track, approve, or monitor overtime, making it hard for both employees and HR to stay on top of things.
The Solution

Designing with clarity
and purpose

The design approach focused on reducing cognitive load at every step — clear visual hierarchy, a task-first home screen, and a consistent component system that scaled across all features.

01
Simplified attendance with face recognition
Designed a one-tap attendance flow with real-time face scan feedback and geofence indicator. Users know exactly where they are and what they need to do — no confusion, no extra steps.
02
Role-based dashboard architecture
Built separate information architectures for admin and employee roles. Admins get a high-level overview with pending approvals; employees see their personal daily tasks and status — all from the same codebase.
03
Progressive disclosure for feature density
Instead of showing all 8+ features upfront, the home screen surfaces the 4 most-used actions with a clear grid. Secondary features are one tap away, reducing overwhelm while keeping everything accessible.
04
Clear approval status system
Designed a consistent status indicator system (pending, approved, rejected) across all request types with color coding and timeline views — so users always know the state of their requests at a glance.
Results

Impact that speaks
for itself

Fixwork launched to real users and quickly became an essential tool in their daily work life. The design decisions made throughout the process directly contributed to adoption and retention.
